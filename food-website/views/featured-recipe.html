Shakshuka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh coriander or parsley for garnish
  • Bread or chapati for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until soft.
  3. Stir in garlic and bell pepper, cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Make small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into each.
  7. Cover the pan and let the eggs cook until whites are set but yolks are slightly runny.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander or parsley.
  9. Serve hot with bread or chapati.
